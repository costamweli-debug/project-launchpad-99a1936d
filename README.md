# ExamPass AI

> Pass NSSCO & AS Level exams with AI.

![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-v1.0.0-success)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-active-brightgreen)
![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-web-blue)

ExamPass AI is an AI-powered learning platform built for Namibian students preparing for NSSCO and AS Level examinations.

Students can practice AI-generated quizzes, upload past papers, chat with an AI tutor, and track their progress—all in one place.

---

## ✨ Features

✅ AI Tutor

✅ AI-generated Quizzes

✅ NSSCO & AS Level

✅ PDF & Image Uploads

✅ Progress Tracking

✅ XP & Streak System

✅ Subject-focused AI Chat

✅ Performance Analytics
