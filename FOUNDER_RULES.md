# Founder Rules

## Rule 1

Students come before features.

---

## Rule 2

Never build a feature without a real user problem.

---

## Rule 3

Every week, speak to students.

---

## Rule 4

Track data, not opinions.

---

## Rule 5

Ship small improvements consistently.

---

## Rule 6

Protect the company's focus.

---

## Rule 7

Every task must answer:

"Will this help us reach 100 active students?"

If the answer is no, don't do it.
